2 Principles

This research is being conducted using the Research Compendium principles10:

  1. Stick with the convention of your peers;
  2. Keep data, methods, and output separated;
  3. Specify your computational environment as clearly as you can.

Data management follows the FAIR principle (findable, accessible, interoperable, reusable)11.
